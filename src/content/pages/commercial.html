Why Commercial Solar

Why Commercial Properties
Invest in Solar

Ontario commercial properties often have large unused rooftops, high daytime electricity usage, and increasing operating costs. Commercial solar systems help businesses reduce long-term hydro expenses while improving property performance.

  • Reduce exposure to rising hydro costs
  • Improve long-term cash flow and NOI
  • Increase tenant satisfaction and retention
  • Improve property competitiveness
  • Support ESG and sustainability goals
  • Offset peak demand charges
  • Use unused rooftop space to generate value
