Main Program
SAVE ON ENERGY
RETROFIT PROGRAM

The Save on Energy Retrofit Program helps Ontario businesses improve energy efficiency, reduce operating costs, and lower greenhouse gas emissions through incentives for eligible energy-efficiency upgrades, including solar photovoltaic systems.

Eligible businesses may qualify for significant incentive support for targeted energy-efficiency retrofits. Applications are subject to program approval and funding availability.

Who Qualifies
ELIGIBILITY
OVERVIEW

Eligible Participants

  • Commercial property owners and businesses
  • Industrial facilities and manufacturers
  • Institutional organizations
  • Agricultural operations and greenhouse facilities
  • Participants must be LDC-billed electricity customers
  • Systems must generally be behind-the-meter
  • Systems greater than 10kW-AC up to 1MW-AC may qualify

Key Requirements

  • Pre-approval mandatory before installation
  • Work must be completed by a qualified contractor
  • Applications require documentation and eligibility review
  • Funding availability may change over time
  • Projects are subject to final approval
  • Program rules may change without notice
