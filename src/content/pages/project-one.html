Home Reno Rebate Program
THE HOME RENO REBATE PROGRAM HELPS YOU SAVE
UP TO $10,000

The Home Reno Rebate Program provides rebates for eligible retrofits, including solar panels and battery storage. Eligible Ontario homeowners may qualify for:

$5K
Solar Photovoltaic (PV) Panels

Up to $5,000 back for eligible solar panel installations. No home energy assessment required for this rebate path.

$5K
Battery Storage for Solar

Up to $5,000 more for eligible battery storage systems. Combined, eligible homeowners may save up to $10,000.

ELIGIBLE RETROFITS INCLUDE

  • Solar photovoltaic (PV) systems
  • Battery storage for solar systems
  • Insulation & air sealing
  • Windows & doors
  • Heat pumps
  • Smart thermostats
  • And more eligible upgrades

Who Can Apply?

  • Ontario homeowners who own and live in their home
  • Households with combined annual income of $250,000 or less
  • Retrofits must be completed by an approved contractor
